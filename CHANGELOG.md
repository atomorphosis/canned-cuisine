# Changelog

## 0.3.0-beta.1

- Added the playable Pressure Canner with atomic batch processing, automation, preview, and persistent inventory.
- Added procedural canned meals with quality, nutrition, effects, naming, appearance, returned cans, and native composition tooltips.
- Added reloadable ingredient profiles, archetypes, and effect rules with bundled Vanilla and optional culinary-mod data.
- Added optional JEI, EMI, Jade, AppleSkin, Tooltip Overhaul, and KubeJS integrations.
- Combined the Pressure Canner preview bars into one complete result tooltip and localized atlas quality requirements by quality band.
- Added curated discovery loot, advancements, English and Spanish localization, and development data diagnostics.
- Hardened canonical composition validation, persistent codecs, atlas synchronization, and integrated-server data cleanup.
- Added minimal and full compatibility builds to continuous integration.
